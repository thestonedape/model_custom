================================================================================
SECTION 3 - FACTUAL INTERPRETATION
================================================================================

1) No practical gain over Replica on test was observed.
   - All compared results remain in the same 29.1 to 29.4 Top-10 band.

2) Turning contrastive off (alpha=0.0) did not produce a meaningful jump.
   - alpha=0.0 slightly improves Top-10 vs alpha=0.9 in this run set,
     but only by a small margin.

3) Current evidence supports "performance saturation for this setup"
   more than "regularization/optimizer deficiency".
   - Strong recipe changes did not materially change test outcome.
