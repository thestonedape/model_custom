- Scheduler in trainer.py: CosineAnnealingLR

6) Why this replica is not improving further right now
- Training plateaus very early (best dev top10 reached early, then little to no gain).
- Current recipe is stable but appears saturated around the 29-30% top10 band.
- Class-frequency bias is strong (the most frequent word dominates top1 behavior in analysis).

7) What should be done next (replica side)
Priority A: make evaluations deterministic and repeatable
- Fix seed protocol and average over multiple seeds for final claims.
- Reason: small differences (<0.2 top10 points) are otherwise noise.

Priority B: run controlled loss-weight sweeps
- Grid alpha in {0.0, 0.05, 0.1, 0.2, 0.5, 0.9} and lambda_vq in {0.0, 0.01, 0.05, 0.1, 1.0}.
- Reason: current setup likely over-weights components that do not improve ranking.

Priority C: mitigate class-collapse pressure
- Try class-balanced CE or weighted sampler.
- Track macro-F1 and per-class top1, not only top-k.
- Reason: top1 collapse can hide behind acceptable top10.

Priority D: lock an ablation matrix
- Baseline only, +VQ, +contrastive, +optimizer/scheduler changes.
- Reason: professor-facing claims should isolate each contribution cleanly.
